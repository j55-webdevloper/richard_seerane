CREDIT & DEBT RELIEF

Running into financial trouble happens to the best of us – but rather than running for the hills or putting our heads in the sand, the best way forward is to confront your debt situation sooner, and put up your hand if you need help.

You are not alone It is alarming to know that the number of consumers listed as having impaired credit records totalled 9.6 million for the quarter ending September 2018. In addition, TransUnion’s Consumer Credit Index for the same period indicated a 2.4% year-on-year increase in consumer defaults. In the constrained economic environment we find ourselves in, this is a warning to consumers to keep a close check on their credit.
