TRACING

In most instances people struggling to locate someone either be a debtor, unclaimed beneficiary or just a missing person and that becomes a nightmare, Now with this in mind, we at Richard Seerane and Associates (Pty) Ltd have a designed a specialized tracing services with a view of not only serving a certain industry but cover many, our specialized services include but not limited to:

Debtor Tracing

This service is a key factor to debt collectors nor creditors as most rely heavily on this activity on their day to day operations to successfully located absconded debtors.

Beneficiaries Tracing

The main objective of an unclaimed benefit fund is to trace beneficiaries and pay out the benefit while limiting the associated costs in the process. We pride ourselves in a high success rate, with no risk to our clients.

Assets Tracing

An asset tracing is the locating of public records that confirm personal property or real estate held by a person or corporate entity. These searches uncover value and any potential liabilities that may be tied to a property. Asset investigations occur daily to help individuals and companies satisfy their due diligence, settle personal injury cases, collect debt, and verify financial status during a divorce or child support case.

To ensure we help our clients reach their objectives we also offer physical tracing services whereby door-to-door tracing will be conducted either at their workplace or located home addresses.
